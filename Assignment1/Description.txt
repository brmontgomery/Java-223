The goal of Assignment 1 was to implement a basic Payroll system. 

The inputs of the application are the employee's name, hours worked, and pay rate.

The outputs of the application are the:
Owed pay to the employee for their normal hours worked (up to 40 hours), 
Owed pay for their overtime hours (time and a half past 40 hours), 
And their total owed pay for all hours worked.
